What would my friends say about me, if you asked? I am a dedicated and ambitious person who always takes on projects wholeheartedly, if I believe in them.

What would my girlfriend say? That this has resulted in 50 liters of mead in the bedroom, 1000 ice cream containers in the basement and that I can explain how the moon affects the tide while I solve a differential equation.

How do I describe myself? That is a tough one. The short answer is that my heart beats especially for two things: to find new and better ways to do things - and become the best I can at what I do.

Through the years I have realised that my full potential is only achieved when I am allowed to use my analytical side and my understanding of complex problems. That is why I feel especially at home in the field of software development - and it is here I see my future.
